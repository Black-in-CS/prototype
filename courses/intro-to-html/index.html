    FAQs

    Who teaches Black in CS's courses?
    All of our courses are taught by experienced instructors and developers who have extensive knowledge and preparation in computer science content and curriculum.
    What technology do I need to take the class?
    In order to have a focus of accesibility for our courses in a technical perspective, we'd recommend good internet connection, as well as a laptop or PC.
    What level of coding should students have?
    Our course has students come in with no coding experience at all and other students who have taken advanced level computer science courses. Thus, our course is labeled as "Beginner - Intermediate" and is designed to meet all students at all levels.
